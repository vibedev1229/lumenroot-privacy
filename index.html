Privacy Policy for Lumenroot

Effective Date: June 29, 2026

Last Updated: June 29, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Lumenroot: Grow the Sky collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile game application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Automatically Collected Information

  • AdMob Advertising: We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect device information, advertising ID, and usage data to show relevant ads.
  • In-App Purchases: Purchases (such as currency bundles and the Season Pass) are processed securely by Google Play Billing. We receive purchase confirmation status only; we do not collect or store your payment details.
  • Game Data: Gameplay statistics, progress, settings, collected Sky Sprites, and unlocked content stored locally on your device.
  • Technical Information: Device type, operating system version, and app performance data.

2.2 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Camera or microphone access
  • Contact list or personal files
  • Social media accounts

3. How We Use Information

The information we collect is used to display advertisements through Google AdMob, process in-app purchases through Google Play Billing, save your game progress and settings locally, and improve game performance and user experience.

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We may share limited technical data with Google AdMob for advertising purposes, and with Google Play Billing to process purchases, subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

5. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Our app is family-friendly but is not specifically directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn that we have collected such information, we will delete it immediately.

6. Data Security

All game data is stored locally on your device. We implement appropriate security measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction of data.

7. Third-Party Services

Our app uses Google AdMob and Google Play Billing. See the Google Privacy Policy at https://policies.google.com/privacy

8. Your Choices

  • Ad Personalization: You can opt out of personalized ads in your device's advertising settings.
  • App Permissions: You can revoke app permissions through your device settings.
  • Data Deletion: Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored game data.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the Last Updated date at the top of this policy.
